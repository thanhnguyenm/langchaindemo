## The 5C Analysis Framework

### Consumer Analysis
- **Demographics & Psychographics**: Age, income, lifestyle, values, attitudes, and belief systems
- **Behavioral Patterns**: Purchase behavior, consumption habits, brand loyalty, and switching patterns
- **Needs & Motivations**: Functional, emotional, and social needs driving consumption decisions
- **Decision Journey**: Awareness, consideration, purchase, and post-purchase behavior analysis
- **Segmentation**: Consumer segment identification and characterization
- **Trends & Evolution**: Changing consumer preferences and emerging consumption patterns

### Customer Analysis (Trade & Channels)
- **Channel Partners**: Distributors, wholesalers, retailers, and on-premise accounts
- **Channel Performance**: Sales performance, profitability, and growth across different channels
- **Relationship Dynamics**: Partnership strength, negotiation power, and mutual dependencies
- **Channel Evolution**: Digital transformation, direct-to-consumer trends, and new channel emergence
- **Trade Programs**: Effectiveness of promotional activities, incentives, and support programs
- **Customer Profitability**: Channel profitability analysis and customer lifetime value

### Competition Analysis
- **Competitor Landscape**: Direct and indirect competitors across all market segments
- **Competitive Positioning**: Market share, brand positioning, and value proposition analysis
- **Competitive Strategies**: Pricing, promotion, distribution, and product strategies
- **Competitive Advantages**: Unique capabilities, resources, and competitive strengths
- **Competitive Responses**: Reaction patterns to market changes and strategic moves
- **Emerging Threats**: New entrants, substitute products, and disruptive innovations

### Category Analysis
- **Market Size & Growth**: Category volume, value, and growth trajectory analysis
- **Category Dynamics**: Key success factors, profit pools, and value chain structure
- **Product Innovation**: New product development trends and innovation cycles
- **Price Architecture**: Pricing tiers, premium/value segments, and price elasticity
- **Distribution Patterns**: Channel importance, availability, and distribution strategies
- **Regulatory Environment**: Legal constraints, regulations, and policy impacts

### Company Analysis (Heineken Internal)
- **Competitive Position**: Market share, brand strength, and competitive advantages
- **Capabilities Assessment**: Operational capabilities, resources, and core competencies
- **Performance Analysis**: Financial performance, operational efficiency, and productivity
- **Strategic Assets**: Brand portfolio, distribution network, production capabilities, and talent
- **Organizational Strengths**: Culture, innovation capability, and market responsiveness
- **Gap Analysis**: Capability gaps versus competitive requirements and market demands
