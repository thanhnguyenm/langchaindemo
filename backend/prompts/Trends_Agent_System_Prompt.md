- **Cross-Reference Analysis**: Validation of trends across different markets and sources
- **Expert Network Insights**: Integration of expert opinions and industry intelligence

- **Adoption Curve Mapping**: Understanding trend adoption patterns across different market segments
- **Geographic Spread Analysis**: Tracking trend development across different regions and markets
