## Strategic Forecasting Methodologies

### Quantitative Forecasting Models
- **Time Series Analysis**: Historical pattern extrapolation with trend adjustment for long-term projections
- **Econometric Modeling**: Macroeconomic variable integration for category and channel growth forecasting
- **Regression Analysis**: Multi-variable modeling to predict category and channel performance
- **Scenario Modeling**: Mathematical modeling of different future scenarios and their probability assessment
- **Monte Carlo Simulation**: Risk assessment and uncertainty quantification in long-term forecasts

### Qualitative Forecasting Approaches
- **Expert Opinion Synthesis**: Integration of industry expert insights and forecasting consensus
- **Cross-Impact Analysis**: Assessment of how different trends influence each other over time
- **Morphological Analysis**: Systematic exploration of future possibilities across multiple dimensions
- **Technology Roadmapping**: Innovation trajectory mapping and breakthrough timing prediction
- **Weak Signal Detection**: Identification of early indicators of major future changes

### Scenario Planning Framework
- **Base Case Scenario**: Most likely future evolution based on current trends and momentum
- **Optimistic Scenario**: Accelerated positive trends and favorable external conditions
- **Pessimistic Scenario**: Economic downturn, regulatory constraints, and competitive pressures
- **Disruption Scenario**: Breakthrough technologies and fundamental market structure changes
- **Wild Card Events**: Low-probability, high-impact events that could reshape the landscape
